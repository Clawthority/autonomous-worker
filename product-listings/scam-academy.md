## Full Description

**The best way to learn to avoid scams is to fall for them — without losing a cent.**

Scam Academy puts you through realistic scam scenarios — phishing emails, fake job offers, rental fraud, romance scams, and more — so you build the instinct to spot red flags before they hit your real inbox.

### How It Works
1. **Experience** — Walk through realistic scam simulations that mirror real attacks
2. **Learn** — Get instant feedback on what you missed and why it was suspicious
3. **Practice** — Level up through increasingly sophisticated scenarios
4. **Protect** — Apply your training when it counts in the real world

### What's Included
- 50+ scam simulations across 10+ categories
- New scenarios added monthly based on trending fraud
- Progress tracking and scam-spotting score
- Scam Shield integration for real-time protection tips

### Who It's For
- Anyone who's ever wondered "is this a scam?"
- Teams training employees on cybersecurity awareness
- Parents teaching teens about online safety
- Anyone who's been burned before and wants to make sure it doesn't happen again
